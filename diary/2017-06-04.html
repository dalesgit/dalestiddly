Day 1 Atlanta {#Day 1 Atlanta}

It is Pentecost. The receptionist yesterday asked us if we were going to church today! I told her that was a first for me. But when I explained about Pentecost and baptism of my children she clearly didn't have a clue.

> Dear children, Everyday I pray for my children, each of you in your own ways are courageously forging the life you are called to live. Generally I add, "The ones who talk to me and the ones who don't." On this, the anniversary of baptism for all of you, I can simply rejoice to remember each of you on the occasion of your baptism. Right now we're in Atlanta and the receptionist, who attends a famous mega-church in the neighborhood, and, while being a passionate Christian in her own style, she didn't have a clue what I was talking about when I shared a little of your story. Today I rejoice and celebrate the life of each one of you. Thanks be to God. P.S.: I would be grateful for those of you who know Emma's contact information that you would pass on this greeting and blessing. Thank you.
