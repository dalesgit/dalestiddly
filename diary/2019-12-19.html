All I want for Christmas {#All I want for Christmas}

We had an exchange yester even after we got home. "What would you like for Christmas?" She said a few things. I thought for a while. It was difficult. But I wanted to take the question seriously.

  • The first thing I said was I wanted to be able to draw. Ever since I was little I wanted to have talent for it. But I didn't.
  • The next thing, similar, I want to be able to play piano. Of course in one sense I've been able to play my whole life. But I've wanted to be able to play it like a musician. Cf. my current struggle to transcribe These Islands.
  • The last thing I said, a simple acquisition kine, was another pair of jeans. Made of stuff that is sort of comfortable to wear.

I included:

  • a storm door like the Dayton house has
  • a fireplace that works
  • live by the ocean
  • to be able to talk to my daughters
