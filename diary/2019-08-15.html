Dreaming

I was doing church work. I was working with groups. Maybe a little the evening with Barb, Sue, and Olga-Maria. They each had their own pastoral universe. Mary Pat had been in charge there. There were, in the dream, competing needs and views of the world.

I wondered as I was waking from the dream whether the focus on church work was a message about what my primary focus ought to be in retirement. Not academia, but the church. I have been cavalier in many ways, not paying attention to the political aspects of the Episcopal Church, dismissing them too easily perhaps. But it has been very good to write them off as in the larger scheme of things not being very important.
