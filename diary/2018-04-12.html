Dream about Mom {#Dream about Mom}

It was so vivid as I struggled to sleep through this night of fits and turns that Mom was alive and kicking and most eager to talk to me. She called while we were on some adventure in the mountains. It really seemed as if Mary Pat was there, but there was climbing, ice and snow, and danger from weather. We were with some people, finally huddled in a cabin or some such. The phone rang and I tried to answer it. All I got out of it was Mom's voice on the other end saying, "Oh, I just don't know how this thing works."

I struggled to find a way to call her back. But it was frustrated with people fighting for the bathroom, some unintended nakedness I think.
