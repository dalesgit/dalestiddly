Dream last night {#Dream last night}

Tue Jan 28, 2020 07:34 AM

I was in a place like Colorado Springs. There was family around but I was alone. I had set out on some task. I had driven the white Kia. It was a familiar route, but somehow changed or I wasn't remembering well. I parked somewhere and walked and walked. Maybe ran a bit. Maybe some other mobility. Did what I needed, I think, more or less. But then trying to get back to where I needed to be. Mary Pat was somewhere north. Somewhere other than where I was. I couldn't quite remember where the car was. I tried and tried. But I couldn't remember where I parked it. I couldn't get back.

It seems as if some friendly folks had taken me, perhaps out of the weather. And finally I called Mary Pat. And she just kind of laughed and conveyed to me that it was my problem and I'd have to deal with it.

We had watched The Notebook and MP had wondered how much of the Alzheimer theme Bill and Karla were actually aware of. I thought they were, but by the end I thought perhaps it was too close to home for them to have enjoyed.

Bill & Karla and I had gone to Jamestown without MP. She was sick and the walking would have been too severe. Then with the preparation for dinner (leftovers), the 3 of us were doing part of the task and it felt like MP was sitting alone, allowing herself to be waited on. Then we didn't do it the way she wanted. Then I lost it because I didn't know all of what was going on. I told her she needed to get involved. Her response was to walk away and attempt to leave me on my own.
