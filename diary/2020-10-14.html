I told her that neither of those was helpful.

I’m awake, now (Wed Oct 14, 2020 06:19 AM), because I was spinning over and over in my head the details I’ve tried to lay out here. I had about 6 hours of sleep. For 3 days now I’ve kept track of my calories. It was easy for me to decline, except for a small bit, the meal Sean had cooked and prepared at her directions without informing me. It’s not the food I need to be eating.

“You say you love me. But your love hurts and marginalizes me.”

The word I was trying to think of days ago, I could only think of “catalyst”, was precipitate (with a short “a”). Sean is the precipitate for all the conflict and hurt going on in the household.

FEAR. I think that what MP did in her response yesterday was to inform me that if I tell her that something hurts me, her response will be, “I don’t care.” It feels like an unsafe place to be. I dare not run, however, because it would leave too much to MP.

It seems over, doesn’t it?
