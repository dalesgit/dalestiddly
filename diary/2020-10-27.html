    This is us

    Beth’s father said to her, “Our lives are built on the tragedies that define our lives.” What do you do with the terrible things? Make lemonade? As it was being spoken I made of it that it is the pillars of the major sadnesses of our lives that our decisions are fashioned into the future. Always we are defending the Maginot line. Two different takes.

    Randall is dealing with “Black lives matter”. His siblings are too oblivious to really reckon with the reality. They are dealing with major events in their lives, as they seek some reconciling act: Kate getting a new baby from adoption, Kevin finding a spouse and a pregnancy with a boy and a girl.

As we watched it seemed unfortunate and somehow “telling” that Sean didn’t want anything to do with watching This is us. All the values important to us that he rejects. The focus is on himself.
