Psalm 31

9 Be gracious to me, O Lord, for I am in distress; My eye is wasted away from grief, my soul and my body also.

10 For my life is spent with sorrow And my years with sighing; My strength has failed because of my iniquity, And my body has wasted away.

11 Because of all my adversaries, I have become a reproach, Especially to my neighbors, And an object of dread to my acquaintances; Those who see me in the street flee from me.

12 I am forgotten as a dead man, out of mind; I am like a broken vessel.

common prayer psalm is dramatic and overwrought, but I identified with it, as I have woke this morning asking myself how could Miriam go to the wedding and not let me know? I shared with MP that it is a similar feeling to reckoning with the fact that a huge swath of the country thinks that racism is ok, mockery of handicapped, etc.

As I read the psalm I wondered whether I should send it to Miriam? Emma? To what end? What do I seek from the two of them?
