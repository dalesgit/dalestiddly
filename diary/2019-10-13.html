Emma

Early in the night I had a vivid dream. I woke from it to pee; then MP woke up and I was able to repeat the dream to her. The setting was a place very like the bedroom we were sleeping in, here at Liki Tiki. There was a sense of someone's presence. I looked around. I moved toward the bathroom and there was someone there, someone being coy I thought. It was Julian. Then I opened the door and there was Emma, enjoying a bath, with bubbles and having a gay old time.

I was upset that she snuck into the house rather than saying something to me. I told her that she would have to leave, but she was always welcome to be in my home if she could talk to me. I felt conflicted.
