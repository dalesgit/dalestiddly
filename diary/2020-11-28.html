What do you want me to say?

The question I can think to ask is: “What could I do that would persuade you to stay in the marriage?”

What I am most afraid of, I think, is that she will say that there isn’t anything.

Dreaming about being in a busy place, like a convention. I was alone and carrying something important. A part of it fell apart into little screws and springs. In a balcony sort of place, spread under the chairs, I was trying to gather the pieces. It was time for me to go (escape?) but I was struggling.

Even as I lay in bed I was wondering about the above words that I had written earlier during a period I couldn’t sleep.
