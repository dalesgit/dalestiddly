Greetings to St Mary's {#Greetings to St Mary’s}

It may seem an odd thing to receive a "greeting" from South Carolina, particularly on the occasion of your appointment as rector of St. Mary's in Honolulu. You've probably already heard something about me. I was rector from 2001-2012. I want to give to you the greatest blessing I can on this occasion, because St. Mary's was one of the greatest blessings of my adult life. I loved the people there with all my heart and I received much love in return. Up to the present day, I tell stories about the amazing acts of God's grace that we shared. I think I can send you greetings, though I am living in South Carolina, because within weeks of my arrival in Hawai'i I felt deep within me that it was home. I still feel that way. I pray that God will bless you as mightily as He has me. Praise be.

Faithfully, The Rev. Dale C. Hathaway

Announcement re. St. Mary's {#Announcement re. St. Mary’s}

ST. MARYʻS ANNOUNCES NEW RECTOR: THE REV. CHARLES BROWNING We are happy to announce the naming of a new priest for St. Mary’s Episcopal Church in Mo’ili’ili. A Florida native, Fr. Charles Browning will be coming to us from St. Andrew’s Chapel and School in Boca Raton, with his wife Cainna and two (soon to be three) children. Fr. Charles is an avid surfer, reader, movie-watcher, collector of vinyl LPs, and fan of science fiction (and especially Japanese giant monster movies). In addition, he is a writer, having had poetry and short fiction published in small-print journals. In 2019, his essay on soteriology, “The Joy of Salvation,” was published by Covenant, the blog of The Living Church. He is also one-third of the Masters of Divinity podcast. E komo mai, Browning family! Fr. Charles begins his call on April 1, 2020.
