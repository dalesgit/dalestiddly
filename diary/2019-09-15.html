Dream

Dream that seemed to go on and on before I woke just before my alarm. It was a church setting. There were new policies, a new vision, being implemented. It had to do with being an inclusive church. Clearly they were successful, but there still seemed to be a challenge at the core. There were two parts to the new policies, one part a radical statement and the second a kind of enumeration of specific situations.

Perhaps I was influenced by the New Amsterdam episode we watched last night where the couple had a child who received a heart transplant. As a family they were unable to afford the tremendous cost of the medications he needed to take to keep from rejecting the new heart. The solution the hospital found was to get a divorce (on paper) and the mother and the child would go on medicare so that the government would pay for the meds. The family objected because they were strict catholics.

Also an influence on the dream was our anticipated visit with the St. Paul's, Monroe this morning. Old friends. Good people. But Kathleen is a follower of Pope Benedict.

Also, it seemed that in my dream I (we?) had young children. There was a kind of camping experience going on. The youngest child seemed to be someone like Julian. Bill talked about seeing Julian as well as Owen on their trip to Colorado. They are leaving today to drive home. Bill seemed possibly interested in flying out here for a visit and then to perhaps Williamsburg.
