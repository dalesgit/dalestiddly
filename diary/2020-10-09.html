  • I have for too long buried my own convictions

Insight (due: 2020-10-09)

It seems clear to me today, mid-day, that the real felt injury for me is that I have been shut out of decision-making with re. Sean.

I would also acknowledge that I share a part of the responsibility for that because I abdicated input, largely because I thought that the parent relationship trumped anything the step-parent thought.

In retrospect it seems that I experienced the primary relationship to be the marriage and that I needed to be more a part of deliberations.

However, I don’t think the pattern we had would allow – and it certainly doesn’t permit now – that I have anything to contribute. Mary Pat’s decisions are final.

There is a pattern of her responses to my thoughts is to explain why they are wrong. She doesn’t recognize that pattern.

All along these months, it seems to me, while she made final decisions, the question could have been asked, “How will Dale’s concerns be addressed?”

So the “forgiveness” is directed at Mary Pat.
