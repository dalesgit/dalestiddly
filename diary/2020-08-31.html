Mary Pat thinks that “Cheap Grace” refers to the charge God puts on the grace. She apologized for not knowing the correct theological terms when I showed exasperation. “Cheap grace” has to do with the price humans put on it. God’s price isn’t cheap, it’s free.

Cheap grace

“Cheap grace is the preaching of forgiveness without requiring repentance, baptism without church discipline, Communion without confession, absolution without personal confession. Cheap grace is grace without discipleship, grace without the cross, grace without Jesus Christ, living and incarnate. –Dietrich Bonhoeffer”

Why not “family?”

In the midst of nap or somewhere, and after hearing Mary Pat seemingly really strike a note against me for not treating Sean as family when I did with so many others, I realized that there was a distinguishing characteristic – a striking one!

In none of the other relationships was one of us in a long-term relationship with the person. Mary Pat has a 42 year relationship with Sean.
