Dreaming about grading

20201006093804 I spent the night tossing and turning and thinking about rubrics and the raft of grades I have to get through in my class. The last thing I looked at was the long list, checking to see how many had turned in the Siddhartha paper. 50 out of ca. 60 now.

Mary Pat had come to bed to read with me. She has made a determined effort to pay attention to me. It has been a little bit jarring, because it’s such a shift from the last few months.

My goal was to take care of myself, cultivate my space. So for me the important thing was that I said, “I don’t want to watch tv.” She joined me after spending a little bit of time with Sean.
