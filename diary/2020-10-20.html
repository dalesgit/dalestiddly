Last night, the blanket pulled off my hind end. It was cold. Pulling the blanket back.

Remembering sleeping bags. First 20+ years lots of that. Being cold.

Mom sent me to Steamboat Springs in summer. To have a father replacement time?

Trotting on the horse down the road and in slow motion rotating of to hit the road.

Peeing in the bag at night. Too cold to get out in the middle of the night? Too ashamed to tell anyone that my bag was soaked.

Thinking there is nobody who cares about those memories. But last night, anyway, they seemed so much a part of me. Or so much some of the pieces of who I am.
