  • His interpretation of Job is wholistic and not historico-critical based. The prologue establishes that Hasatan is a functional officer, like CIA. 3 friends go on and on as if to illustrate that their dogmatic sermons are empty. Job maintains that they deny his experience. Epilogue: God provides the ultimate experience -- a theophany -- and Job is made little in its place. The closing, however, where Job receives everything back in spades, is a reversal.
