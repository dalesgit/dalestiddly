Barbara Bush RIP at 92 {#Barbara Bush RIP at 92}

Her dying has been anticipated for a couple of days. She was always in my memory a positive image in a family that in other ways seemed so tarnished. A tough woman. A matriarch. Lots of boys. And so she reminded me of Mom.

And Mom is dying at 92. Though Bill reports that Charlene reports that she did better yesterday. So typical of Mom's saga.

But I think that deeper down than I have gone in recent weeks, the anticipation of her death is sinking in. Not with regrets. With sadness. With poignance. With a feel for some of the "might-have-beens." Quite a sense of loss I guess. But also an awareness that I got much of my sense of love from the woman. For better or worse.
