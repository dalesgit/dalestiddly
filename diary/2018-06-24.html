I have a long day ahead of me and still I couldn't sleep. Some was ruminating about +Bob. Some not.

I am really thinking of the character Rudolph Abel in the movie we saw the other night, []{#Bridge of Spies}Bridge of Spies. When repeatedly asked if he was worried, he responded, "Would it help?" in the calmest most Buddhist way.

+Bob is a rascal, koloho, is that right? But he doesn't have peace. I repeated to myself that I have cut out the main things that I made sure I pursued in Hawai'i: counsel, walking, writing.
