I feel so alone. Sean has bought his car. And I feel like I’ve cut myself off. But like the actors on “Prom” I feel all alone. Mary Pat and Sean have their world and I’m not part of it.

I feel like this is now Mary Pat and Sean’s household. I’m not a part of it.

More than that, this evening, I felt like I wasn’t myself. Partly that I didn’t know myself. But that I had lost myself. Mary Pat was the boss, making all the decisions, and the Sean episode has demonstrated that I take third fiddle. Where am I?
