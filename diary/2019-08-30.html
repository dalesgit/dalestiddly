Prayer group {#Prayer group}

Sue, Barb, Olga-Maria, Mary Pat, {#Sue, Barb, Olga-Maria, Mary Pat,}

This is an update-report on the prayer-group meeting yesterday that was attended by Mary Pat, Olga-Maria, and myself. As we had talked about at the meeting at our house a few weeks ago, the purpose for yesterday's meeting was to decide on some of the specifics for going forward with the group. Our time together at the WELCM house was prayerful and I think grace-full. We came to several conclusions about the immediate future of the group:

  • The place and time for the meeting will be the WELCM house, 4:30 pm on Thursdays.
  • The format will be a simplified lectio or African Bible study method.
  • the meeting will be open to students and hopefully it will become a part of the student's "programming".

One of the reasons for re-imagining the group as a part of the programming for the campus ministry was that we would need the ministry's governing board's approval to meet there. We each had an intuitive sense that it was "appropriate" at several levels, that a kind of "radical hospitality" was at the center of all prayer and especially prayer groups, and that we all grow from relationships that are outside our norms.

The next meeting will be 4:30 pm, Sept. 5 at WELCM house.
