Preaching

In my dreaming before getting up I was []{#so energized}so energized! I was preaching. Or maybe just putting to paper my sermons. It grew out of reading some of my past sermons as I was trying to figure out the best workflow. Perhaps shifting to Joplin.

But one thing came through. A tremendous enthusiasm for just preaching what I knew -- out of the current events of my real life experiences.

So I was enthused about 4 Advent.

  • inexpensive, inexperienced movie people (the little people like me)
  • movie that didn't kill metaphor, kill imagination
  • the importance of little people from the perspective of the universe
  • We couldn't talk about God or God's work without metaphor and imagination
    • God is love -- not the kind of love I'm most associated with
    • Happy days -- not the holidays I know
    • Angels that look like cherubs -- "angels often come disguised as teachers" -- but they always are disguised
    • God in the silence -- allowing space for imagination
    • even contemplation which clears out our imagination to let God in

And Christmas Day. The Gretchen Pritchard piece.

  • Telling of stories is what it's all about
